About Us

My name is Takura Shoko, and I was born in Zimbabwe. I have spent the past two years working in the IT industry as a web designer, during which I have gained extensive experience and proficiency in various languages and tools used in web design. My journey in the tech world has been both challenging and rewarding, allowing me to hone my skills and expand my knowledge. Recently, I transitioned to a full-stack developer role, which has given me the opportunity to delve deeper into both front-end and back-end development. This new phase in my career excites me, as it allows me to integrate my design expertise with comprehensive development capabilities to create more robust and dynamic web applications.
